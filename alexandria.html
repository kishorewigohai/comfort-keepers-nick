Detailed Care Services Overview

Personal Care and Companion Care

Important personal tasks such as grooming, hygiene, mobility assistance, transferring, positioning, and meal preparation are at the heart of our personal care services. We ensure seniors receive careful assistance with daily tasks while preserving their dignity and comfort. Our trained caregivers also provide meaningful companionship — from engaging conversation to shared activities — that enriches everyday life and combats isolation.

24-Hour and Live-In Care

Some seniors require more extensive assistance — whether due to complex medical conditions, limited mobility, or other needs that demand continuous support. We offer 24-hour care and live-in care for seniors who need around-the-clock attention. Our caregivers provide seamless, uninterrupted care that ensures safety, comfort, and peace of mind for both the senior client and their loved ones.

Alzheimer's and Dementia Care

When your loved one suffers from a cognitive disorder, it can be difficult to administer care on your own. Navigating the complexities of Alzheimer's and dementia requires specialized care and a gentle touch. Our caregivers are well trained and experienced in providing compassionate support that promotes safety, routine, and comfort — creating a familiar environment that helps reduce anxiety and confusion.

Post-Hospital Care

When seniors are discharged after a hospital stay, it can be challenging to care for themselves at home alone. Our post-hospital care services provide dedicated support during the critical recovery period — including medication reminders, meal preparation, mobility assistance, and transportation to follow-up appointments — all designed to reduce re-admission risk and promote faster healing.

In-Home Respite Care

For friends and family who provide care to their loved ones, we offer respite care services. Our caregivers will step in so you can take a break, run errands, or simply recharge — knowing your loved one is in compassionate, capable hands. Whether you need a few hours or a few days, our flexible respite care ensures continuity of quality care.

Veterans Care

Our veterans have sacrificed so much for our country, and they deserve the highest quality of care. Comfort Keepers is proud to offer specialized in-home care services for veterans in Alexandria. We assist veterans and their families in navigating VA Aid and Attendance benefits, which can help cover the cost of in-home care. Our caregivers understand the unique needs of veterans — from mobility challenges and chronic conditions to emotional support for those dealing with PTSD or other service-related concerns. We are committed to honoring their service by providing dignified, respectful, and compassionate care in the comfort of their own home.

In-Facility Care

If your loved one resides in a senior care facility, our caregivers can provide extra personalized care to them there as well. We offer supplemental one-on-one attention, companionship, and assistance that complements the facility's existing care — ensuring your loved one receives the individual focus and comfort they deserve.
